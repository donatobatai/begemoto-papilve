Experiential Marketing Events

While traditional advertising (radio, print, television) verbally and visually communicates the brand and product benefits,
experiential marketing tries to immerse the consumers within the product by engaging as many other human senses as
possible. In this way, experiential marketing can encompass a variety of other marketing strategies from individual
sampling to large-scale guerrilla marketing. The goal of experiential marketing is to form a memorable and emotional
connection between the consumer and the brand so that it may generate customer loyalty and influence purchase
decision. Services for experiential include:

  • Experiential Strategy and Planning
  • Conceptual Creative and Experience Design
  • Immersive Brand-led Events
  • Roadshows
  • Exhibitions
  • Shopping Centre Tours
  • Festival Events
  • Sampling

Experiential marketing. If brands to communicate their messages effectively while engaging in traditional marketing mix activities it is incremental to transfer positive experiences to customers in order to create desired brand image and foster communication. Word of mouth is one of the best types of advertising allowing to shape product associations that could be leveraged by inclusion of active social media campaign.
